*For those who loved in times that had no word for it,*
*and for the ones they could not say.*

In the last great age of sail, a young Norwegian named Olav Hestby left the island of his birth to find out what kind of man the sea would make of him. He came home, in time. He was never again the boy who had sailed.

These are the watches he kept.
