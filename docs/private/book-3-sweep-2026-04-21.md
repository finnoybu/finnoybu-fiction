## Canon violations (should fix)

### 1. [Ch 29:10](../../content/en/book-3/29-the-churchyard.md#L10) — Olav's birthday propagated as March

> *"He was eighty years and six months."* (Sept 1938)

September 1938 minus 80y 6m = **March 1858** birth. Contradicts [Ch 1:146 banns](../../content/en/book-3/01-hestby.md#L146) (*"twenty-four years and seven months"* on March 14, 1883 → **August 1858**) and [Ch 25:28](../../content/en/book-3/25-haakon-in-a-paper.md#L28) (Olav "fifty-two" in March 1911 → only consistent with August 1858 birth; March 1858 would make him 53).

This is the propagation of the known [Book II Ch 30 "six days after my birthday"](../../content/en/book-2/30-the-churchyard.md) March-birthday flag into Book III. Expansion-proposal explicitly warned against propagating; Ch 29:10 predates that warning.

**Fix:** `"eighty years and six months"` → `"eighty years and one month"` (Aug 1858 + 80y 1m = Sept 1938). Alternatively, `"eighty"` to stay neutral.

### 2. [Ch 25:26](../../content/en/book-3/25-haakon-in-a-paper.md#L26) — Off-by-one retirement age

> *"his retirement from the *Helga* in the autumn of 1910 at the age of fifty-one"*

Autumn 1910 minus 51 years = autumn 1859. Doesn't match either birth-month candidate. If born August 1858, autumn 1910 = 52. If born March 1858, autumn 1910 = 52. **Age should be fifty-two, not fifty-one.**

This also creates an internal contradiction with the same chapter: [Ch 25:28](../../content/en/book-3/25-haakon-in-a-paper.md#L28) has Olav "fifty-two" in March 1911 — consistent with a fifty-two-year-old who retired five months earlier, not a fifty-one-year-old.

**Fix:** `"at the age of fifty-one"` → `"at the age of fifty-two"`.

### 3. [Ch 28:22](../../content/en/book-3/28-the-widower-years.md#L22) — "Eighty-four" age stray

> *"He was sixty-nine at the moment he registered that the trade of his working years was entirely gone. Eighty-four was not an age at which a man mourned. It was an age at which a man observed."*

Prior sentence places Olav at 69 (matches 1927 Houlder office closure, born August 1858). The "eighty-four" in the following sentence does not correspond to any depicted moment in Book III — Olav's final on-page age is 80 in [Ch 29:10](../../content/en/book-3/29-the-churchyard.md#L10) (Sept 1938); he does not appear at 84. Reads as a typo.

**Fix:** `"Eighty-four"` → `"Sixty-nine"` (preserves the intended aphorism).
